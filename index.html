Who We Are

Midwest Roots,
Nationwide Routes

Faster Transport Inc is an authorized for-hire interstate carrier headquartered in Cedar Rapids, Iowa — right in the heart of the Midwest's freight corridor. We move everything from general freight and refrigerated goods to heavy machinery and agricultural products.

Our operation is built on a simple principle: every load matters. With a lean fleet of professional drivers and a hands-on dispatch team, we deliver the kind of personal attention that larger carriers can't match. Our perfect inspection record isn't a fluke — it's the result of daily commitment to safety and maintenance.
